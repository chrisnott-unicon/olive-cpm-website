The Development Context

Remote Sites.
Total Control.

Executing major infrastructure across Southern Africa comes with inherent risks. Geographical isolation, fragmented supply chains, extreme weather events, and delayed site reporting plague traditional contracting.

For developers and Principal Agents managing capital from major hubs, overseeing a project in a deep-rural or cross-border node often feels like looking into a black box. Olive CPM was built to shatter that black box.

Contractual Defence

By enforcing rigid, timestamped daily site records, Olive actively builds a defensive wall of data against unfair penalties and secures valid Extensions of Time (EOT).

Live Site Operations

No more waiting for month-end PDF reports. Stakeholders gain immediate, cloud-based access to site weather data, active labour counts, material deliveries, and progress tracking.

Platform Architecture

Core Modules

Designed for the reality of the African construction site. Olive features a clean, intuitive interface that requires near-zero learning curve for site agents and foremen.

Statutory Site Records

The heartbeat of the platform. Olive digitizes the traditional site diary, forcing rigorous daily compliance. It captures critical data points required to back up JBCC and GCC claims with irrefutable evidence.

  • Daily Operations: Log progress and instructions.
  • Automated Weather: Live GPS atmospheric records.

Programme Integrity

Keep your critical path visible. Olive acts as a strategic scheduling hub, ensuring the Baseline Programme is constantly updated against daily site realities to forecast delays before they trigger penalties.

  • Milestone Tracking: Granular activity monitoring.
  • Slippage Prediction: Identify bottleneck risks early.

Integrated Documentation

Never build off an outdated revision again. Olive creates a single, controlled document register, seamlessly linking with cloud storage nodes to synchronize the entire professional team.

  • Cloud Sync: Integrates with Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive.
  • Controlled Registers: Centralized Drawings & RFIs.

Executive Contract Dashboard

A broad, immediate view of the project's macro details. Aligning the entire stakeholder group on commercial contract formats, timelines, and the professional roster at a glance.

  • Central Roster: Rapid access to Principal Agent details.
  • Time Logistics: Live tracking of contractual completion dates.
