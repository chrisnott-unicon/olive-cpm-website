The Development Context

Remote Sites.
Total Control.

Generic software fails on the African construction site because it relies on manual data entry. Olive CPM flips the paradigm: the application is driven entirely by your initial contract inputs and geographic coordinates.

For developers and Principal Agents managing capital from major hubs, overseeing a project in a deep-rural or cross-border node often feels like looking into a black box. Olive CPM was built to shatter that black box.

Automated Telemetry

Lock in your site's GPS coordinates once. Olive automatically pulls localized atmospheric and weather data directly into your statutory daily diaries.

Contractual Defence

By enforcing rigid, AI-audited daily site records, Olive actively builds a defensive wall of data against unfair penalties and secures valid Extensions of Time (EOT).

Platform Architecture

Intelligent Modules

Designed for the reality of the site office. Clean, intuitive interfaces powered by complex AI routing behind the scenes.

Contract-Driven Setup

Everything begins with the agreement. Upload your GCC, JBCC, or FIDIC contract to automatically initialize project milestones, set compliance baselines, and trigger automated contractual notifications.

Statutory Site Records

The heartbeat of the platform. Olive digitizes the site diary, capturing critical data points (labor tracking, geo-fenced weather, QR-coded material deliveries) required to back up claims with irrefutable evidence.

AI-Powered Procurement

Bridging the BOQ with the supply chain. AI parses your Bill of Quantities and cross-references your project's GPS location to generate and route RFQs to localized supplier directories automatically.

Finance & Certification

Generate accurate interim valuations and professional payment certificates. Olive maps site progress directly against your imported BOQ, ensuring payment timeframes are strictly adhered to.

Programme Integrity

Keep your critical path visible. Our planning module leverages AI to analyze daily progress against the baseline, accurately forecasting delays and bottlenecks before they trigger penalties.

Compliance & Audit

Automated structural health and regulatory oversight. The AI Assistant constantly audits site records against National Building Regulations and the OHS Act, flagging vulnerabilities instantly.

Enterprise
Architecture.

Olive CPM is designed to function as an extension of your own brand. Operating as a multi-tenanted ecosystem, the platform grants your firm absolute administrative control over data, users, and security.

  • White-Label Output

    Upload your corporate logo and credentials. Every generated RFI, site instruction, and payment certificate carries your branding, not ours.

  • Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)

    You control the roster. Assign specific permissions to site agents, principal engineers, and client representatives on a per-project basis.

  • Secure Native & Dual Storage

    Secured via Firebase and native Google Workspace authentication. Keep data natively in Olive, or seamlessly mirror your project repositories to your own external cloud drives for absolute data sovereignty.
